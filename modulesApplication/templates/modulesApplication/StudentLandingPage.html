Landing Page

Message to Designers:

  • This page will be the landing page for the students.
  • It is up to you whether you would like to have separate landing pages for the different user types or not.
  • Be careful when renaming files if you choose to have one big landing page with different features for different users. File names are closely linked to code in views.py and urls.py.
  • The landing page(s) are where your NavBars will really come in handy.
  • From here, a student should be able to view their modules, select their modules (Link to a new page), view all modules (Link to a new page) and more that may be implemented in future sprints.
  • Don't expect your landing pages to look entirely complete. Though they should look aesthetic, there is more functionality that we are likely to need and come across later.

If you want to have a page that one of the buttons on the landing page can link to, this will be the link for students to choose their modules.
